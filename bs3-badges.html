Badges & Labels

Badges

Badges are numerical indicators of how many items are associated with a link.

The .badge class is applied to span within an anchor or button element:

News 5
Comments 10
Updates 2

Labels

Labels are used to provide additional information about something.

The .label class is applied to a span element, followed by one of the six contextual classes:

  • .label-default Default Label
  • .label-primary Primary Label
  • .label-success Success Label
  • .label-info Info Label
  • .label-warning Warning Label
  • .label-danger Danger Label
